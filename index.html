About us

The Poopsie People, formerly known as Drew Alford Official, is a YouTube channel that makes videos of sketch comedy. The most well-known of which is the Oopsie Poopsie series, which is where the name came from. There is currently no upload schedule, we upload whenever we finish a project. We start new projects usually after the last one is uploaded. There is no definite upload date for whatever we upload. Please be patient with us, we ensure quality content!
